Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At TopDividendTools.com, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information we collect. This Privacy Policy explains what data (if any) we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal, non-personal information automatically through tools like Google Analytics (e.g., anonymized page views, browser type, approximate location, referral sources). This helps us understand how visitors use the site and improve it.

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information (name, email, IP in identifiable form, etc.) unless you voluntarily provide it — for example, by emailing us or submitting a tool via the "Submit Tool" form.

2. Use of Information

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Analyze site performance and user behavior
  • Improve content, rankings, filters, and user experience
  • Respond to inquiries or tool submissions

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (via Google Analytics) to track usage patterns and enhance functionality. These are mostly first-party and analytics cookies. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, though doing so may limit some site features (e.g., filters remembering state).

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics (anonymized usage data)
  • Google Fonts (font loading)

These services may collect anonymized data under their own privacy policies. Please review Google's privacy policy for details.

5. Sponsored & Affiliate Content

Some tool listings may be sponsored (clearly labeled with "SPONSORED" badges) or contain affiliate links. These do not affect how we handle your privacy — no additional tracking is added beyond standard site analytics.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data we hold. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
